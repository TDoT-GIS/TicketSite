To gain access to Esri E-Learning you will need a public Esri account. If you do not already have an account you will need to create one. After creating an ESRI account, click here to put in a Helpdesk ticket. Make sure to include the email used to create your ESRI account. You will then recieve an email inviting you to access e-Learning on the Esri training website.
